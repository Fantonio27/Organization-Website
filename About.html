About Us

Founded on January 18, 2022, M&H is among the most promising

and influential agency and family in Bigo Live Philippines.


History

This was created last year, because we saw and we experienced different families in BIGO that is not a happy environment for us, some are toxic, some are destroying each other just because of the supporters. With this, our agency and family envisioned that any members of this family will no longer experience bitterness, loneliness but to have joy, happiness, sweetness and abundance on which milk & honey symbolically represented.

Mission

In all we do, our goal is to build trusted relationships, deliver positive culture and create memorable experiences beyond expectations. We aim to nurture and hone the talents and skills at the same time push everyone to develop new skills and to showcase their full potential.
